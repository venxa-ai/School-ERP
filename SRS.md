# 1. Project Overview

The School ERP is an AI-powered education management platform designed to digitize school operations, automate academic and administrative workflows, improve communication among students, teachers, parents, and administrators, and provide real-time insights for better decision-making.

---

# 2. Project Objectives

- Digitize school administration
- Improve academic management
- Automate attendance and reporting
- Simplify fee management
- Enhance parent communication
- Integrate AI into daily school operations
